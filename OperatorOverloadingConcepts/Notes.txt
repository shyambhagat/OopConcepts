Operator_Overloading Project Notes

Overview
Demonstrates operator overloading in C# using a Customer class.

Key Operator Overloads

- + Operator (Wealth Addition)
  - Adds the wealth of two customers.

- - Operator (Wealth Subtraction)
  - Subtracts the wealth of one customer from another.

- > and < Operators (Wealth Comparison)
  - Compares the wealth of two customers to determine who is wealthier.

- + Operator (Address Merging)
  - Merges additional address details into a customer’s address.

Application
This example simulates real-world operations like adding, subtracting, and comparing customer wealth, as well as merging address details, all through operator overloading.
